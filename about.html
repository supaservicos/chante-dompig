CHANTÉ-MARY

DOMPIG

Chanté-Mary Dompig (born 12 February 2001) is a Dutch football player. In 2017, she started with the reserve team of VV Alkmaar.  In 2020, Dompig signed a contract with Empoli to play football in Italy. In 2022, she signed a contract with AC Milan until 2025...


FULL NAME: Chanté-Mary Dompig
DATE OF BIRTH: EARLANDS
NATIONALITY: THE NETHERLANDS
POSITION: ATTACKER


Chanté Dompig is all smiles. The Dutch player is one of those players that every team needs: she participates, gets involved, exudes positive energy. She is a team player and is always available, behaves impeccably with respect, and smiling is just a way of life. She uses it in presenting herself in general, to break the ice and joke of course, but it is also the most beautiful way to create bonds and spirit. It is a form of leadership, spontaneous and essential, to deal with the dynamics on football field, for example.


She inspires when things are going well; she gives strength and courage when things are not going so well, like now. The Rossonere's season is struggling to get going. They are in a sticky patch; the encouraging opening game against reigning champions Roma - a 2-4 loss - has not been followed up by much afterwards. It was against the Giallorossi that Dompig scored her first and only goal so far this league campaign. She is a striker who gets big goals: the first in AC Milan 3-3 Juventus in April, the second in Fiorentina 1-1 AC Milan in May.
